#VarBULLET
---+++ BULLET -- bullet sign
   * Bullet sign, useful to create a bullet list in a TWiki table, such as =| %<nop>BULLET% One %<nop>BB% Two %<nop>BB% Three |=

   * Syntax: =%<nop>BULLET%=
